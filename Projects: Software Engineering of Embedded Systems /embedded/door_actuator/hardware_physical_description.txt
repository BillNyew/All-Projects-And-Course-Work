Connect microcontroller to the board, powered through USB. 
Connect SIG terminal(1) of potentiometer to D0
Connect VCC terminal(2) of the potentiometer to 3.3V 
Connect GND terminal(3) of the potentiometer to GND
Connect PWM pin of servo motor to D1
Connect positive terminal of servo motor to 3.3V 
Connect negative terminal of servo motor to GND
