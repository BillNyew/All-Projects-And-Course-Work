FORGE Exciting Things Ahead

We’re constantly building new tools and resources to support men and veterans on their mental health journey. Here’s a sneak peek at what’s coming to Forge!

Upcoming Features

  • AI Avatar (Early Access): Talk with a visual, voice-powered companion for even more natural conversations—designed for privacy and empathy.
  • Speech Recognition: Speak instead of type, hands-free. Get instant support wherever you are.
  • Advanced Progress Tracking: Visual charts, weekly summaries, and personal growth tips—see your wins over time.
  • New Group Modules: Veteran-only rooms, career-focused groups, and peer mentorship opportunities.
  • Mobile App: All the power of Forge in your pocket (iOS & Android).
Watch a sneak peek of what’s coming to Forge!

Our Roadmap

  1. Q3 2025: Beta test AI avatar & speech support with premium members
  2. Q4 2025: Launch progress analytics and new community groups
  3. 2026: Release the Forge mobile app and open mentor network

Want to help us shape these features? Sign up below to be an early tester or send us your ideas.

Why Are We Building This?

Many men—especially veterans—prefer to talk, not type. Our AI avatar and speech recognition aim to make Forge even more approachable, supportive, and stigma-free. Every new feature is designed based on real feedback from our community.
