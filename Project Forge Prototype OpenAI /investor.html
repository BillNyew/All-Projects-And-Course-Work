Executive Summary

Forge leverages AI, secure communities, and therapist networks to break stigma and get men the support they need—at scale. We serve a high-need, often overlooked market: men, especially veterans, who want confidential, stigma-free help on their terms.

Our Impact & Market

  • 70M+ men in the US alone struggle with mental health issues every year
  • 2x more likely for men to avoid therapy or support compared to women
  • Veterans at even higher risk—PTSD, isolation, suicide rates far above average
  • Forge meets them where they are: private, digital, and ready 24/7
