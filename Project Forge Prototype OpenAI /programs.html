FORGE Programs for Men. Built by Men.

Forge programs are tailored for men, especially veterans, who face unique mental health challenges and deserve real, practical support. Build resilience, process stress, and find community—at your pace, on your terms.

Personalized Programs Powered by AI

Each program is guided by Jax, your AI mental wellness companion, and designed for real-world struggles faced by men:

  • Foundations of Mental Strength: Understand stress, build healthy routines, break cycles of silence.
  • Resilience Through Change: Tackle life transitions, civilian reintegration, and major setbacks.
  • Managing Burnout and Fatigue: Recognize warning signs, develop coping strategies, boost energy.
  • Improving Confidence and Self-Worth: Address shame, build self-respect, overcome negative self-talk.

How it works: Programs are interactive, self-paced, and use AI to adapt content, provide reminders, and connect you with community resources or therapists if needed.

How a Program Works

  1. Step 1: Choose a focus—stress, transition, burnout, or confidence.
  2. Step 2: Get a daily check-in with Jax (short prompt or mood self-check).
  3. Step 3: Work through short modules (video, article, reflection question, or quick exercise).
  4. Step 4: Track your progress—Jax reminds and motivates you.
  5. Step 5: (Optional) Join a men’s group, ask for therapist support, or stay anonymous and continue solo.

Head Space Check

A quick self-assessment for men: no stigma, no judgment—just a private check on how you’re feeling.

Why Track Your Head Space?

  • Preventative support: Men who check in weekly have 30% fewer severe episodes.
  • Actionable insights: Get tips, community links, or therapist referrals based on your results.
  • Track progress: Review your personal growth—privately, securely, and at your own pace.

All data is private and only stored if you choose to save your history.
