FORGE Features That Make a Difference

Explore the technology and design behind Forge’s support for men and veterans. Every feature is built to remove barriers and make getting help easier, safer, and more effective.

What Sets Forge Apart?

  • AI-Powered Chat (Jax): Private, always-on support for men—share thoughts or get instant tips 24/7.
  • Personalized Programs: Interactive modules built around men’s real struggles, guided by Jax, at your pace.
  • Veteran & Men’s Community: Anonymous forums and local groups tailored for men and former service members.
  • Therapist Matching: AI-matching to counselors with veteran experience, flexible and private sessions.
  • Head Space Check: Easy mood check-ins, progress tracking, and early warning for stress or burnout.
  • Privacy & Security: End-to-end encryption, anonymity, HIPAA-compliant standards.

How the Technology Works

  1. Smart AI (Jax): Uses secure language models to guide, support, and connect men with resources (never stores private chats unless you choose to save).
  2. Anonymous Community: All posts are de-identified; AI-powered moderation keeps forums safe.
  3. Therapist Finder: Our system cross-matches your needs with real humans, based on experience with men’s and veterans’ issues.

User Journey

  1. Check in with Jax about your stress or a tough week.
  2. Try a quick “Head Space Check” and see suggestions tailored for you.
  3. Join a men’s group, post in a forum, or ask Jax to recommend a human therapist.
  4. Book a session, keep your notes private, and track progress in your own time.
