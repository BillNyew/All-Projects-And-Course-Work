FORGE Find Strength in Others

Welcome to a judgment-free, anonymous community space built for men—especially veterans—who are looking for real support, honest conversations, and a sense of belonging.

What Makes Forge Different?

  • Veteran and men's groups: Topic-based rooms for stress, grief, relationships, and transition after service.
  • Anonymous and safe: No names, no pressure—share honestly and get support.
  • Peer support: Offer advice, listen, and help other men who get it.
  • AI-powered moderation: Keeps discussions respectful and safe for everyone.

Community Features

  • Anonymous Posts: Share your story without revealing your identity.
  • Topic Filters: PTSD, anxiety, fatherhood, career, identity, and more.
  • Local Groups: Find men near you for wood shop, fishing trips, bowling, or camping.
  • Activity Scheduler: Create or join events, group chat, and reminders—all optional.
  • AI-Powered Safety: Automatic flagging and removal of unsafe content keeps everyone protected.

How to Get Started

  1. Step 1: Register or sign in—anonymity is always an option.
  2. Step 2: Choose a topic room or enter your ZIP code to find local groups.
  3. Step 3: Post a discussion, reply, or just read and listen.
  4. Step 4: Schedule or join group meet-ups (in person or virtual).
